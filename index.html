A unique student project in Antwerp

We are pleased to offer you this small-scale investment project of 16 student rooms, located in the vibrant heart of the university area of ​​Antwerp.


Antwerp, the second largest student city in Flanders after Ghent, currently has more than 500,000 inhabitants and welcomes more than 60,000 students. The demand for student rooms in the city continues to rise year after year. For example, in 2023, 10% more students chose a room than the year before. However, this growing demand has not led to a similar increase in supply, resulting in both a qualitative and quantitative shortage of student housing.


Centre.1 is a stylish building that is equipped with all modern comforts and meets the contemporary standards and requirements of the modern student. It offers an ideal solution for investors who want to contribute to tackling the acute shortage of student rooms.
At Centre.1 you not only opt for an investment in a triple A location in the heart of the student area, but also for an energy-efficient building that meets the needs of modern times. Moreover, Centre.1 offers a small-scale and well-organized approach, without having to invest in a large complex.
